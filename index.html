NES Legend of Zelda: Recreation + Custom Level

Click here to play

Use WASD or the arrow keys for movement. X for sword, Z for alt weapon. Space to switch between equipped alt weapons. Press 4 for the custom level.

This project was created using C# in the Unity game engine. With my project partner, I recreated the first dungeon of the NES Legend of Zelda game, and added a custom level introducing a dash mechanic and 2 new bosses. I implemented functionality for the sword, bow, enemy movement and logic, and game audio. In our custom level, I created Browser (the second boss) and all of his mechanics. I also helped with the dash mechanic.
